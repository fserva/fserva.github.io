Note: to programmatically download multiple files from zenodo restricted access repositories, you will need the token generated by the website.
First do curl -c zenodo-cookies.txt "https://zenodo.org/record/{record number}?token={your token}" to generate a cookie file, then download your file(s) of interest with curl --cookie zenodo-cookies.txt "https://zenodo.org/record/{record number}/files/{target file name}.zip" --output {target file name}.zip.
Just change every field in curly braces {} with the relevant string for your case.
